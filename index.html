About Mia

Mia Kulinchenko is a vocalist, bassist, and performer from Oakville, Ontario. With a voice that cuts through genres — from raw rock power to smooth soul — she brings intensity and authenticity to every stage she touches.

As the lead vocalist and bassist of Whitenoise, she performs alongside guitarist Adam Musmar and drummer Dennis Kulinchenko. The trio formed in 2022 and has been creating and performing together across the GTA since.

She also fronts After Dark Band, playing live venues throughout Toronto. Whether it's an intimate open mic or a packed club, Mia's performances stay with you.

Currently studying at York University. Available for live events, studio sessions, and collaborations.
